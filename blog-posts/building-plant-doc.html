Project Overview

Plant diseases can significantly affect crop quality and agricultural productivity. Many farmers and plant owners may not always have quick access to expert advice when symptoms appear.

The goal of Plant Doc was to create an application that helps users identify potential plant diseases quickly and conveniently using technology. By combining artificial intelligence with an easy-to-use interface, the application provides users with valuable information about plant health.

AI-Powered Disease Detection

One of the key features of Plant Doc is its ability to analyze plant conditions through images. Users can upload a photo of an affected plant, and the system analyzes visible symptoms to identify possible diseases.

Image-based detection makes the application accessible even for users who may not know the technical names of diseases or symptoms.

Text-Based Analysis

In addition to image analysis, Plant Doc also supports text-based disease identification. Users can describe symptoms such as leaf discoloration, spots, wilting, or unusual growth patterns.

The application processes the provided information and suggests possible plant health issues based on the symptoms described.

Combining both text and image analysis allows the application to offer a more flexible and user-friendly experience.
