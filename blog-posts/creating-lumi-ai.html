Lumi AI is one of the most personal and creative projects I have worked on. The idea started from a simple question: what if an AI assistant felt more like a companion than a traditional chatbot?

Instead of creating another standard chat interface, I wanted to build an interactive AI companion with its own personality, expressions, and animations. The result was Lumi AI, a pixel-art assistant designed to make conversations feel more engaging and enjoyable.

The Idea Behind Lumi

Most AI chat applications focus entirely on functionality. While they are useful, many of them feel robotic and lack personality.

I wanted to explore a different approach by creating an assistant that users could connect with visually and emotionally. Lumi was designed to feel like a digital companion that reacts, responds, and interacts with users in a friendly way.

The project combines artificial intelligence with character design, animation, and user experience to create something that feels more alive than a typical chatbot.

Designing The Companion

One of the most exciting parts of the project was designing Lumi itself. I chose a pixel-art style because it gives the character a unique identity and nostalgic charm.

Lumi features multiple animated expressions and emotional states. Depending on interactions, the character can appear happy, surprised, confused, thinking, excited, or curious.

These visual reactions help create a more engaging experience and make conversations feel less mechanical.

User-Owned AI Integration

A major design decision was allowing users to connect their own AI API keys instead of relying on a built-in paid service.

This approach gives users more flexibility and control over which AI model they want to use while keeping the project accessible and affordable.

Users can simply provide their own API credentials and immediately begin interacting with Lumi through the application.

Features Implemented

  • AI-powered conversations.
  • Pixel-art animated companion.
  • Multiple emotional expressions.
  • Dynamic companion reactions.
  • User-provided API support.
  • Modern chat interface.
  • Responsive design.

These features work together to create an experience that feels closer to interacting with a companion than simply using a chatbot.

Development Journey

Developing Lumi required combining multiple areas of software development, including frontend design, animation systems, API integration, and user interaction design.

One challenge was ensuring that the companion's visual behavior matched the overall conversation experience. The animations needed to feel natural and responsive without distracting from the chat itself.

Another challenge was designing a clean interface that balanced functionality with character-focused interactions.

What I Learned

Lumi taught me that great software is not only about solving problems but also about creating memorable experiences.

Through this project, I improved my understanding of AI integration, user experience design, animation systems, and interface development.

I also learned how powerful visual storytelling can be when building digital products.

Future Plans

Lumi is a project I would love to continue expanding in the future.

  • Voice-based conversations.
  • Additional companion personalities.
  • Custom companion appearance options.
  • Memory and personalization features.
  • More advanced emotional reactions.
  • Expanded AI provider support.

These additions would help Lumi become an even more interactive and personalized companion experience.

Final Thoughts

Lumi AI represents the combination of my interests in artificial intelligence, design, and interactive experiences. More than just a chatbot, Lumi is an experiment in making AI feel friendly, expressive, and approachable.

It remains one of my favorite projects because it allowed me to combine creativity and technology to build something uniquely my own.
